## What this app does

This app adds a **storefront chat widget** to your Shopify store and a **Shopify admin app** where you can:

- Turn the widget on/off
- Customize the widget look (colors, button, popups, quick actions)
- Train the assistant using your store content (knowledge base)
- Review chats and customer requests
- Track analytics and manage plans

---

## What customers see

- A chat button (launcher) on your storefront
- A chat window where they can ask about products, policies, and orders
