## Recommended setup order

### 1) Customize the widget

- Open **Appearance**
- Adjust colors, button, greeting message, popups, and quick actions
- Click **Save settings**

### 2) Train the assistant with your store content

- Open **Settings → Knowledge base training**
- Add:
  - your policy pages (returns, shipping, warranty)
  - FAQs (manual entries)
  - relevant documents (size guides, care instructions)

### 3) Configure how order verification works

- Open **Settings → Order lookup**
- Choose whether customers must provide:
  - email
  - phone
  - email or phone

### 4) Test it like a customer

- Open your storefront in an incognito window
- Ask a few questions:
  - “What’s your return policy?”
  - “Do you have this in size M?”
  - “Where is my order?” (see what it asks for)

### 5) Review your inbox and requests

- Open **Chats** and **Requests**
- Confirm you can:
  - view conversations
  - update statuses
