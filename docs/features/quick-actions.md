## What are quick actions?

Quick actions are buttons inside the chat that help customers do common actions faster.

Examples:

- “Track my order”
- “View size guide”
- “Contact support”

---

## Where to configure them

- In the app, open **Appearance → Quick actions**

---

## Action types

- **Message**
  - Clicking the button posts a message into chat (and the assistant responds).
  - Best for: “Track my order”, “Return policy”, “Show bestsellers”.

- **URL**
  - Clicking opens a link you provide.
  - Best for: account page, shipping policy, contact page, booking page.
