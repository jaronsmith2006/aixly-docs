## Where to find it

- In the app, open **Settings**

---

## Order lookup (verification)

When customers ask about orders, you can require:

- Email only
- Phone only
- Email or phone

This helps prevent sharing order details without verification.

---

## Tone, style, and personality

You can tune:

- Formal vs conversational tone
- Response length (concise vs detailed)
- Humor level
- Emoji preference

You can also set:

- Assistant name
- Advanced personality instructions (brand voice and rules)

Best practices:

- Keep instructions short and brand-safe.
- Avoid sensitive or restricted topics in personality instructions.

---

## Custom routing

Custom routing suggests a link when a customer asks about a topic.

Examples:

- “Book a test ride” → booking page
- “Store location” → locations page
