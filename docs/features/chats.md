## Where to find it

- In the app, open **Chats**

---

## What you can do

- Browse conversations by category tabs:
  - All, Complaints, Compliments, Sales-related, Support-related
- Search by user/message
- Open a conversation thread
- Mark status:
  - Open / Waiting human / Closed

---

## Tips

- Review **Complaints** daily.
- Close resolved threads to keep your inbox clean.
