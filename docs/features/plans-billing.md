## Where to find it

- In the app, open **Plans**

---

## Starter plan (free)

The Starter plan may include a monthly conversation limit.

If you reach the limit:

- The storefront widget can pause/hide until your period resets

---

## Paid plans

Paid plans are approved through Shopify’s normal billing flow.

After you select a plan, Shopify will ask you to confirm the subscription.

---

## Usage and resets

Your Plans page shows:

- Current usage for the billing period
- The date your plan period resets

---

## Usage charges (overage)

If your plan includes overage billing, usage charges may appear in:

- Shopify Admin → Settings → Billing
- The app’s Analytics page (Usage charges section)
