## Widget not showing on the storefront

### Step 1: Confirm the embed is enabled

- Shopify admin → **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
- **App embeds** → enable **Aixly AI Chatbot** → **Save**

### Step 2: Use the Setup Guide inside the app

- App → **Dashboard → Overview**
- Complete:
  - Enable storefront widget
  - Verify launcher on storefront

### Step 3: Check Appearance settings

- App → **Appearance → Button**
- Make sure **Show launcher** is enabled.

### Step 4: Wait and refresh

Sometimes changes take a short time to propagate:

- Wait 1–2 minutes
- Refresh the storefront again

---

## Widget disappeared after working earlier

Most commonly this happens when:

- Your **Starter plan limit** was reached for the month.

Fix:

- App → **Plans**
- Upgrade, or wait until the plan resets.

---

## Knowledge base training failed

### URLs

- Use policy and help pages on your own store domain.
- Avoid pages with little readable text (mostly images).

### Documents

- Use PDF/DOC/DOCX/TXT.
- Keep the file size under the limit shown in **Settings → Knowledge base training**.

---

## Answers feel inaccurate

Improve accuracy by:

- Adding/refreshing key policy pages (returns/shipping/warranty)
- Adding manual FAQs for your top 10 questions
- Reviewing **Settings → Tone & personality** to match your brand voice
