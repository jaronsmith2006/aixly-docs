## Installation & setup

### How do I enable the chat widget on my store?

- Shopify admin → **Online Store → Themes → Customize → App embeds → enable Aixly AI Chatbot → Save**
- App → **Dashboard → Overview → Setup Guide** can guide you to the theme editor.

### How do I confirm the widget is working?

- Open your storefront in a new tab and refresh once.
- In the app’s Setup Guide, “Verify launcher on storefront” should complete.

## Appearance & behavior

### Can I change the chat button position, label, or icon?

Yes — app → **Appearance → Button**.

### Can I change colors and greeting text?

Yes — app → **Appearance → Colors** and **Appearance → Layout**.

### Can I add Quick actions?

Yes — app → **Appearance → Quick actions**.

---

## Knowledge base

### How do I train it with my policies?

App → **Settings → Knowledge base training** → add policy URLs and FAQs.

### Why are some URLs rejected?

For safety, only your store’s own domain (or subdomains) is allowed.

## Plans & limits

### Why did the widget disappear?

Most commonly: the Starter plan monthly conversation limit was reached. Check **Plans** in the app.
